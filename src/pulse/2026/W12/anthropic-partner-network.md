## What happened

Anthropic launched the Claude Partner Network on March 12 with a $100 million investment commitment for 2026. The program provides training, technical support, and joint market development for organizations helping enterprises adopt Claude. Anthropic is scaling its partner-facing team fivefold, offering dedicated Applied AI engineers and technical architects for partner deals, and introducing its first technical certification: Claude Certified Architect, Foundations. Membership is free and open to any organization bringing Claude to market.

## Why it matters

This signals Anthropic's push beyond developer adoption into enterprise sales channels. The $100M commitment and 5x team scaling indicate serious intent to compete with OpenAI and Microsoft's established enterprise partnerships. The certification program (Claude Certified Architect) mirrors patterns from cloud providers and could create a professional ecosystem around Claude integration, similar to AWS or Azure certifications.

- Systems integrators and consultancies building AI solutions
- Enterprise architects evaluating Claude for production deployments
