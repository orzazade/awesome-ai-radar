## What happened

Lightricks released LTX 2.3, a 22-billion-parameter Diffusion Transformer model that generates synchronized video and audio in a single forward pass. The model supports resolutions up to 4K at 50 FPS and can produce clips up to 20 seconds long. It is open-source, lowering the barrier for video generation research and application development.

## Why it matters

Previous open-source video models either produced low-resolution output or required separate audio synthesis pipelines. LTX 2.3 generating both 4K video and synchronized audio in one pass closes a significant gap between open-source and proprietary video generation tools. The 50 FPS output and 20-second duration make it practical for short-form content creation workflows.

## Who should pay attention

- Creative tool developers building video generation features into their products
- Content creators and marketing teams exploring AI-generated video for short-form content
- Researchers working on multimodal generation, particularly joint audio-video synthesis
