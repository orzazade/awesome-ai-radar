## What happened

Apple plans to allow third-party AI chatbots to integrate with Siri in iOS 27, according to a Bloomberg report on March 26. Users with apps like Claude, Gemini, or other AI assistants installed will be able to route Siri queries to those services through a new "Extensions" setting in the Apple Intelligence section. This breaks OpenAI's current exclusive ChatGPT integration, opening the platform to any AI provider that enables support in iOS 27.

## Why it matters

This is the first time Apple is creating an open integration layer between its voice assistant and competing AI models. For AI companies, it represents a major new distribution channel — direct integration into the default assistant on over a billion devices. For developers, it signals that AI assistant interoperability is becoming a platform-level concern, not just an API design choice.

- Frontend developers working on conversational AI interfaces
