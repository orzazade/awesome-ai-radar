## What happened

Anthropic launched computer use capabilities for Claude Cowork on March 24, enabling the desktop agent to open apps, navigate browsers, fill spreadsheets, and interact with on-screen elements autonomously. Combined with the Dispatch feature, users can now message Claude a task from their phone and the agent completes it on their Mac while they are away, creating a persistent cross-device conversation thread between Claude Desktop and Claude mobile.

## Why it matters

This marks a significant step in AI agents moving from chat-based assistance to autonomous desktop automation. The cross-device Dispatch model — where you assign work from mobile and the agent executes on desktop — introduces a new interaction paradigm for knowledge workers. Unlike browser-only agents, Cowork operates at the OS level, potentially replacing multi-step manual workflows.

## Who should pay attention

- Knowledge workers looking to automate repetitive desktop tasks
- Developers building agent tooling or competing desktop AI products
- Enterprise teams evaluating AI-assisted workflow automation
