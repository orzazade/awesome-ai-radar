## What happened

Google released Lyria 3 Pro on March 25, an advanced music generation model from DeepMind that creates tracks up to 3 minutes long, a 6x increase from Lyria 3's 30-second limit. The model understands musical composition structure, allowing prompts for intros, verses, choruses, and bridges. Lyria 3 Pro is available to paid Gemini subscribers, in public preview on Vertex AI for enterprise use, and integrated into Google Vids for Workspace customers. All outputs are watermarked with SynthID.

## Why it matters

The jump from 30 seconds to 3 minutes with structural awareness moves AI music generation from novelty demos to potentially usable production assets. The Vertex AI availability makes it accessible to developers building multimedia applications at scale, while SynthID watermarking addresses growing concerns about AI-generated content provenance.

## Who should pay attention

- Developers building creative or multimedia applications
- Content creators and marketers exploring AI-generated audio
- Enterprise teams using Google Workspace or Vertex AI
