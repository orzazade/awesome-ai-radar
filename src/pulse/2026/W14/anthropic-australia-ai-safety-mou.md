## What happened

On March 31, 2026, the Australian government and Anthropic signed a memorandum of understanding for AI safety and research cooperation. This follows Anthropic's announcement of Sydney as its fourth Asia-Pacific office (March 10) and continues Anthropic's pattern of government engagement on AI safety.

## Why it matters

Government-industry AI safety partnerships are becoming a regular pattern. For developers, these MOUs often precede procurement decisions and regulatory frameworks that affect how AI products can be deployed in government and regulated sectors within the region.

- Policy-focused developers tracking AI governance trends
