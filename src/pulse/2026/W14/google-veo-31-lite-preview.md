## What happened

Google launched Veo 3.1 Lite Preview on March 31, 2026, its most cost-efficient video generation model. Available through the Gemini API as `veo-3.1-lite-generate-preview`, the model is designed for rapid iteration and high-volume video generation applications. It replaces the deprecated `gemini-2.5-flash-lite-preview-09-2025` model.

## Why it matters

A cost-efficient video generation model accessible via API lowers the barrier for developers experimenting with AI video in products. The "Lite" designation suggests Google is building a tiered video generation offering similar to their text model lineup (Pro, Flash, Flash Lite).

## Who should pay attention

- Developers building video content tools or creative applications
- Teams evaluating AI video generation for product features
- ML engineers comparing video generation model costs across providers
