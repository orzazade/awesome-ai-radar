## What happened

On April 2, 2026, OpenAI acquired TBPN (Technology Business Programming Network), a daily live tech talk show hosted by John Coogan and Jordi Hays on YouTube and X. The show reportedly generates over $30 million annually. TBPN will maintain editorial independence and report to OpenAI's chief political operative Chris Lehane. This is OpenAI's first acquisition of a media company.

## Why it matters

An AI company acquiring a media property signals a new phase in the AI industry's relationship with public discourse. While TBPN retains editorial independence, the acquisition raises questions about how AI companies will shape the narrative around AI development. For the developer community, it indicates that AI companies are investing heavily in influence beyond their core products.
