## What happened

Starting April 2, 2026, teams on ChatGPT Business and Enterprise can add Codex-only seats with pay-as-you-go pricing — no fixed seat fee, billed on token consumption. OpenAI also lowered ChatGPT Business annual pricing from $25 to $20 per seat and is offering up to $500 in credits for new Codex team members.

## Why it matters

Token-based billing gives engineering teams better cost visibility and removes the barrier of committing to per-seat pricing for an AI coding tool that may see variable usage across team members. This pricing model aligns Codex more closely with how API-based tools are typically billed.

## Who should pay attention

- Engineering managers evaluating AI coding tool costs
- Teams currently on ChatGPT Business or Enterprise plans
- Developers comparing Codex pricing against Claude Code and Cursor
